DiaWolf

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 26, 2026  ·  Last Updated: June 26, 2026

Byte Cycles Studio LLC ("we," "us," or "our") built DiaWolf as a native iOS and Apple Watch application for people with diabetes who want to track workouts alongside continuous glucose monitor (CGM) data. This policy explains what data DiaWolf accesses, how we use it, and what we never do with it.

Short version: Your health data stays on your device and in Apple HealthKit. We never sell it, share it with advertisers, or send it to data brokers — ever.

1Data We Access

Health & Fitness Data (Apple HealthKit)

DiaWolf reads and writes the following HealthKit types to track your workouts:

  • Workout sessions — type, duration, distance, active energy
  • Heart rate and heart rate variability
  • Step count and walking/running distance
  • GPS route data during outdoor workouts

Glucose Data (Dexcom CGM)

With your explicit authorization, DiaWolf connects to the Dexcom API to fetch real-time and historical glucose readings, trend direction, and device information. This connection uses OAuth 2.0 — we never see your Dexcom username or password.

Data You Enter Manually

  • Manual glucose readings (finger-stick or manual CGM entry)
  • Bike and equipment profiles
  • App preferences and display settings

Location

DiaWolf requests location access during active outdoor workouts to record your route. Location is not collected in the background outside of an active workout session.

2How We Use Your Data

We use your data exclusively for health and fitness purposes directly related to DiaWolf's mission:

  • Displaying live workout metrics and CGM glucose overlays during exercise
  • Storing and summarizing completed workouts
  • Calculating Glucose Management Indicator (GMI) estimates from CGM history
  • Generating exportable workout files (GPX, video)
  • Syncing workouts to third-party services you explicitly authorize
We do not use HealthKit or CGM data for advertising, marketing, analytics sold to third parties, or with data brokers. This is both our policy and a requirement of Apple's HealthKit guidelines.

3Data Storage & Security

  • Health and workout data is stored on your device via Apple HealthKit and CoreData.
  • Authentication credentials (Dexcom, Strava, Coros tokens) are stored in your device's secure Keychain.
  • If you enable the optional backend sync feature, data is transmitted over encrypted HTTPS to your personal server instance.
  • We do not operate shared cloud servers that store your health data.

4Third-Party Integrations

DiaWolf connects to these services only when you explicitly authorize each connection. You can disconnect any service at any time from DiaWolf Settings.

Service Purpose What's Shared Privacy Policy
Dexcom CGM glucose readings OAuth token only; glucose data is fetched directly to your device dexcom.com
Strava Export completed workouts Workout duration, distance, route, activity type strava.com

5Apple HealthKit

DiaWolf's use of Apple HealthKit is governed by Apple's developer guidelines. In compliance with those guidelines:

  • HealthKit data is used only for health and fitness purposes within DiaWolf.
  • HealthKit data is never used for advertising or shared with data brokers.
  • HealthKit data is never shared with third parties without your explicit, action-initiated consent (e.g., exporting a workout to Strava).

You control all HealthKit permissions from iOS Settings → Health → Data Access & Devices → DiaWolf.

6Your Rights & Choices

  • HealthKit permissions — iOS Settings → Health → Data Access & Devices → DiaWolf
  • Location permissions — iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Location Services → DiaWolf
  • CGM connection — DiaWolf Settings → Disconnect Dexcom
  • Third-party connections — DiaWolf Settings → disconnect Strava, Coros individually
  • Delete all app data — delete the DiaWolf app from your device. HealthKit data you authored can be managed separately through the Apple Health app.

7Children's Privacy

DiaWolf is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

8Changes to This Policy

For material changes, we will provide notice within the app. Continued use of DiaWolf after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
