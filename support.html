Support

Questions, bugs, or feedback — start here.

Questions, bugs, or feedback? Email support@diawolf.net — it's read by the person who builds DiaWolf, and you'll typically hear back within 2-3 business days.

Before you email, a lot of common questions are answered below.

Glucose & CGM

Which CGMs does DiaWolf work with?

DiaWolf reads glucose data through Apple Health, so it works with any CGM app that shares data to Apple Health — including Dexcom G6/G7, FreeStyle Libre 2/3, and Eversense 365.

Is my glucose data private? Where does it go?

Your glucose and health data stays between your device, Apple Health, and (if you enable iCloud backup) your own private iCloud account. DiaWolf doesn't sell health data or use it for advertising. See the full Privacy Policy for details.

Why isn't my glucose reading updating?

Make sure DiaWolf has permission to read Blood Glucose data in Settings > Privacy & Security > Health > DiaWolf. If you're using Apple Watch, glucose updates on the Watch face can lag by a few minutes when idle — this is intentional (it avoids draining your battery by waking the Watch constantly) and updates as soon as you raise your wrist.

Workouts & Activity Tracking

What workout types does DiaWolf support?

Cycling, running, hiking, walking, treadmill, indoor biking, swimming (pool and open water), strength training, and several others — plus most standard HealthKit activity types.

Does DiaWolf work with my bike sensors?

Yes — DiaWolf supports Bluetooth speed and cadence sensors, including Wahoo and Garmin sensors. You can also save your bike and wheel size as a profile for accurate speed/distance.

Can I track strength training — sets, reps, weights?

Yes. DiaWolf logs sets and reps per exercise, tracks personal records, and includes rest timers between sets.

Does DiaWolf show my glucose during a workout?

Yes — that's the core of what DiaWolf does. It overlays your glucose trend alongside heart rate, pace, and distance in real time, and shows how your glucose responded up to two hours after you finish, since that's the window that matters most for post-exercise lows.

Apple Watch

Does DiaWolf have an Apple Watch app?

Yes. You can start and track workouts from your Watch, see live heart rate and glucose, and view HR zones — all without needing your phone nearby.

Can I start a workout from my phone and still see it on my Watch, or vice versa?

Yes, workouts started on either device mirror to the other automatically.

Subscription & Pricing

What's included in DiaWolf Pro?

Pro unlocks extended history, deeper glucose-exercise trend analysis, GMI/eA1c convergence panels across multiple time windows, route replay, and custom app themes.

What are the pricing options?

DiaWolf Pro is available as:

  • Monthly — $8.99/month
  • Annual — $59.99/year (discounted vs. monthly)
  • Lifetime — $129.99 one-time purchase

How do I cancel or manage my subscription?

Subscriptions are billed through your Apple ID and can be managed anytime in Settings > [your name] > Subscriptions on your iPhone. DiaWolf doesn't handle billing directly — Apple does, so cancelling there is all you need to do.

Is there a free trial?

Yes — new subscribers get a 14-day free trial that enables all Pro features immediately, so you can try everything before committing.

Data & Backup

What happens to my data if I get a new phone?

If you have iCloud backup enabled, your workouts and settings restore automatically when you sign into the same Apple ID on a new device. Your health data itself lives in Apple Health, which already syncs via iCloud independently of DiaWolf.

Can I export my workout data?

Not yet from within DiaWolf directly — that's planned for a future update. In the meantime, your workout and health data already lives in Apple Health, which has its own built-in export feature you can use today.

Troubleshooting

My workout didn't show up in History.

Make sure the workout finished and saved (check Apple Health directly — if it's there, it should sync to DiaWolf). If it's missing from both, try force-quitting and reopening DiaWolf.

I'm not getting low/high glucose alerts.

Check that Notifications are enabled for DiaWolf in iPhone Settings, and that your alert thresholds are set the way you want under DiaWolf's Settings > Glucose Display.

The app feels slow or a screen looks wrong.

Please email a description (and a screenshot if it's visual) — this kind of report is genuinely the most useful thing you can send, since it's hard to catch every device/iOS combination before release.

Feedback & Feature Requests

How do I request a feature or report a bug?

Email support@diawolf.net with as much detail as you can — what you expected, what happened instead, and your device/iOS version if it's a bug. DiaWolf is built by one person, so every piece of feedback is actually read and considered, even if it takes a while to act on.
